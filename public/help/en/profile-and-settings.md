# Profile and settings

Your personal preferences and details live on the **Profile** screen. Open it by clicking **Profile** in the left menu (under the **Account** heading). Account-wide map settings live in a separate panel, described at the end.

At the top of the Profile screen you see your **name** and **email address** next to your profile picture. Below that are three cards, left to right: **User Profile**, **Profile Information**, and a card listing your **Roles** and **Policies**.

## Appearance, language, and the compact menu

The **User Profile** card holds your personal appearance and language preferences, under an **Account** heading:

- **Light/Dark Mode** — turn the switch on for a dark theme, off for a light theme. The change is applied immediately.
- **Sidenav Mini** — turn this on to keep the left menu in its slim, compact form.
- **Language** — open the dropdown and choose your language. TrackHub ships in English and Spanish, shown in the list by their codes (**en** and **es**). The screen switches straight away.

Click **Save** to remember your choices. Your saved theme, menu, and language preferences are restored automatically each time you sign in.

## Editing your profile

The **Profile Information** card shows and lets you update your personal details:

- **Username** and **Email Address** — shown for reference (read-only).
- **First Name** — required.
- **Second Name** — optional.
- **Last Name** — required.
- **Second Surname** — optional.
- **Date of Birth** — optional.

Edit the fields you want to change, then click **Save**. If a required field (**First Name** or **Last Name**) is empty, TrackHub highlights it and does not save until you fill it in.

## Changing your password

1. Open **Profile** from the left menu.
2. In the **Profile Information** card, click the small **lock** icon in the top-right corner of the card.
3. The **Update Password** window opens.
4. Type your new password in the **Password** field.
5. Type the same password again in the **Confirm Password** field.
6. Click **Save**.

**Password requirements.** Your new password must be at least **8 characters** and include **an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter and a number**, and the **Password** and **Confirm Password** fields must **match exactly**. If any rule is not met, TrackHub shows a message and does not save until it is corrected. Adding a symbol makes it stronger still.

## Your roles and policies

The third card is read-only. It has two sections:

- **Roles** — the roles you belong to (such as **Administrator**, **Manager**, or **User**).
- **Policies** — the access policies you belong to (named groupings such as **Full Access**, **Manage Users**, **Read Only**, **Limited Update**, and **Audit**).

This tells you what you are allowed to do; you cannot change it here. For what each role unlocks, see [Roles and permissions](topic:roles-and-permissions).

## Account settings (the gear button)

If you manage your account, a round **gear (settings)** button appears in the **bottom-right corner** of the screen on any page. Clicking it slides open a panel titled **Settings** (with **Account Settings** shown beneath), where you adjust map-related options for everyone in your account:

- **Maps** — choose the map provider: **OSM** (OpenStreetMap) or **Google**.
- **Maps Key** — only appears when you pick a provider other than OSM; enter the provider key here.
- **Online Interval (Min.)** — how many minutes without a report before a unit is treated as offline.
- **Refresh Map** — turn automatic map refreshing on or off.
- **Refresh Map Interval (Seconds)** — how often the map refreshes when the switch above is on.

Click **Save** to apply. A message reminds you that **some settings may require a page refresh to take effect**.

This gear button and its settings panel are only available to Managers and Administrators. If you do not manage the account, you will not see the gear button.
